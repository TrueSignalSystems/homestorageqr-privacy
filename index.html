Privacy Policy for Home Storage QR

Effective Date: 22 April 2026

Home Storage QR is developed and operated by True Signal Systems.

1. Overview

Home Storage QR is a local-first mobile application designed to help users manage physical storage boxes using QR codes. The app does not require user accounts, logins, or cloud connectivity.

2. Data Collection

Home Storage QR does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to any external servers.

All data is stored locally on the device, including:

  • Box/container information
  • Location notes
  • Photos added by the user
  • QR label data

3. Data Sharing

Data is only shared when the user explicitly chooses to export or share it (e.g., snapshot files or QR labels).

4. Camera and Photos

The app may access the camera and photo library. All photos remain on-device unless the user chooses to share them.

5. In-App Purchases

Payments are handled securely by Google Play. The app does not process or store payment data.

6. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Play Billing only. No analytics or ads are used.
